The core idea
Every customer is different.
The message should be too.
AI makes that decision — for every single one.

Within any selected prospect audience, different individuals respond to entirely different things — different emotions, value propositions, visual layouts, and product terms. That variance isn't random. It's predictable.

The question isn't which message tests best on average. The question is: which message is best for this specific individual? That's a decisioning problem — and it's what AIgnyte was built to solve.

Existing tools observe, test, or route. AIgnyte decides. It encodes the creative, models the individual, and makes the match — automatically, every campaign cycle.

The old approach

"Which message performs best on average for this segment?"

A/B tests a small pool of creatives across large subgroups. Takes months. Picks a winner for the segment. Ignores individual variation. Leaves 20–70% of potential lift untouched.

→
The AIgnyte approach

"Which message is optimal for this specific individual?"

Scores every creative against every individual's unique Preference Profile. Makes a distinct decision for each of the millions of people in your campaign file. Every cycle. Automatically.

Genetic Signature — Decoding the creative

AIgnyte analyzes every element of every creative asset in your catalog — the envelope, the letter, the headline, the offer, the layout — and decomposes it into thousands of precise data points across four dimensions. This encoding happens once per creative and persists across campaigns.

Stage Two
Preference Profile — Decoding the individual

Not everyone responds to the same message. A 25-year-old college student responds very differently to a credit card offer than a 45-year-old professional. AIgnyte builds a Preference Profile for every single person in your campaign — a precise picture of what each individual is most likely to respond to, based on who they are, what they've done, and how people like them have behaved in past campaigns.
