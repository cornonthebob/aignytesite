The Problem

Message Decisioning is the only thing enterprise marketing hasn't optimized.

01

Manual, intuition-driven selection

Message choice is made by humans using gut feel and convention. Slow, inconsistent, and impossible to scale.

02

A/B tests are expensive — and wrong for individuals

They require large holdout populations, significant investment, and weeks of wait time. And when they finish, the winner is optimal for the average person. Every individual who isn't average gets the wrong message.

03

Campaigns compound bad decisions

The same suboptimal message assignments repeat cycle after cycle. Each bad decision compounds into the next.

04

Rising CAC makes it critical

When customer acquisition costs rise, even small message improvements become financially transformative across millions of records.

Technology

Three-stage process. Drop-in integration. Continuous learning. See full details →

1 Genetic Signature

Decode the creative

AIgnyte analyzes and decomposes each creative asset — capturing emotions, value propositions, product terms, and visual elements in thousands of data points. Every creative gets a precise Genetic Signature.

2 Preference Profile

Predict each target's preferences

Using campaign history, customer attributes, and past behaviors, AIgnyte builds a Preference Vector for each individual — a personal preference score for every element of every Genetic Signature.

3 Match to Individual

Select the optimal creative

For each person in each campaign, AIgnyte matches their Preference Vector to the Genetic Signatures of eligible creatives and selects the best fit — automatically, at scale, every campaign cycle.
